Welcome to Rio Salado

a distinctive residence

Nestled between Tempe Town Lake, 'A Mountain', ASU Stadium and the lightrail, Rio Salado sits within an urban neighborhoor that has grown into a vibrant, unique mid-city community that is everything you need and everything you want.

Luxury was of great consideration in the development of both projects - the home and community. It's here that you'll find the best of both world - private, resort-like vacation accomodations in the heart of the city. Thoughtfully designed and luxuriously appointed, Rio Salado will feel and function like an extension of your home. It's here, where you can't help but relax and recharge.
